Designing the W5 Summit

  • Collaborators: Meghan Lo | Li Hai Zheng
  • Ongoing project

    • W5 strives to cultivate an inclusive entrepreneurial community, promoting the entrepreneurial mindset of hustle, passion and elasticity towards failure. Through the mentorship of the Accelerator, to open competition events such as the W5 Summit, W5 designs these initiatives to support budding entrepreneurs in any stage of their innovations as well as those interested in start up culture.

    As a Director of Marketing on team, I am responsible for spearheading promotional content for W5 to improve event outreach. I also work collaboratively with the marketing team and the Vice-President of Marketing to build W5's brand image through promotional campaigns. W5 Summit in specific is W5's flagship event. It features a hackathon styled 3 hour case competition encouraging students to innovation a solution to address one of the areas of: waste management, social wealth imbalance or mental health.
