Designing the PepsiCo Case Competition

  • Collaborators: Jacqueline Shi | James Quinlan | Daniaal Mahmood
  • Completed: Nov 24, 2018

    • PBSN PepsiCo Case Competition is a first and second year case competition at the University of Western Ontario. The event was sponsored by PepsiCo and challenged students to develop a solution for PepsiCo products. The event garnered hundreds of applicants as competing teams fought for the generous prize money and a potential interview position.

    As a media executive on team, designing the campaign for this case competition was an incredible creative roller coaster. The case competition was one of PBSN's largest first semester events. Our media team was tasked with:

    • Creating media assets and marketing initiatives which focus on selling the scale and calibre of the case competition rather than convincing students to attend
    • Curate a marketing angle to showcase our esteemed sponsor (especially with an increasing number of students inquiring for more details about the case competition, it gives the Media team a major opportunity)

    PepsiCo Branding Research

    Based on research, PepsiCo's branding was filled with explosive colours, dynamic and unpredictable forms. They exhibit an aura of youthfulness and constant innovation.

    Initial inspiration

    Inspired by PepsiCo, we based our initial teaser draft on similar round fluid forms. Our colour scheme consisted of yellow and orange hues + bold and navy blues.

    Final Marketing Material

    After conducting market research, we note a falling trend in Facebook users amongst our chosen demographic of first and second year university students. Given this observation, our marketing campaign focused on capitalising on our instagram following and incorporating a video promotion aspect to engage viewers.

    Here's the rough breakdown of our hollistic promotional campaign:

    • Teaser graphic
    • Facebook event page banner graphic
    • Instagram 3 part puzzle post
    • Instagram story post
    • Snapchat filter
    • Promotional video

    Promotional Video

    Reflection

    Overall, this campaign was successful and resulted in 3000+ total Instagram post impressions. Our instagram story campaign also effectively garnered 150+ views each. Moving forward, I believe that the promotional material could be further varied. Through incorporating after effect animations and motion graphics, PBSN could further elevate the range of promotional material and encourage higher engagement.
